Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

This application ("the App") is a personal collection tracker tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

App Data

The App stores your game collection records (profiles and collection progress) directly in your own Google Drive using the restricted drive.appdata scope. This data is:

  • Stored exclusively in your personal Google Drive
  • Never transmitted to or stored on our servers
  • Only accessible by you and this App

We do not collect, process, or store any personal information on our own servers.

Google Authorization Data

The App only asks for the Google Drive drive.appdata permission needed to save and sync your collection data. We do not request Google account profile access, such as your email, name, or profile photo.

To keep your sign-in state during use, the App stores an OAuth access token and token expiry time locally in your browser. This authentication data is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Analytics Data

This website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to collect anonymous traffic statistics, including:

  • Page views and visit duration
  • Referring URLs and geographic region (country level)
  • Device type and browser type

Cloudflare Web Analytics does not use cookies and does not identify individual users. All data is aggregated and anonymous. For more information, please refer to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Google API Usage

This App uses the Google Drive API with the drive.appdata scope solely for the purpose of storing and syncing your game collection data across devices. We do not access any other files in your Google Drive.

Our use of Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to any third party. Your collection data remains in your own Google Drive and is never accessed by us.

Data Deletion

To delete your App data, you can:

  1. Remove the App's access from your Google Account permissions page
  2. Delete the App's files directly from your Google Drive appdata folder

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.
